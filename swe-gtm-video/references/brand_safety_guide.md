# Brand, Legal, and Trust Guide

## Brand asset handling

Company logos, product logos, intro/outro animations, icons, lockups, and brand marks must be treated as locked source assets.

Never:

- Regenerate a logo with an AI model
- Ask an image/video model to recreate a logo
- Recolor or distort brand marks unless brand guidelines explicitly allow it
- Place logos over unsafe contrast or busy backgrounds
- Use unapproved customer or partner logos

Always:

- Use provided source files
- Keep aspect ratio intact
- Use approved clear space
- Log brand assets in the asset registry

## Product truth

Never imply:

- The product supports features not verified
- AI agents perform actions without approval if that is untrue
- Customer outcomes, ROI, or time savings without evidence
- Compliance/security guarantees beyond approved language

## Privacy

Never show:

- Customer names or privileged matter/client information
- Emails, phone numbers, tokens, API keys, internal URLs, account IDs
- Confidential docs or financial/legal/health records
- Production admin screens unless specifically approved and sanitized

## Review standard

A beautiful video that damages trust is a failed video.
