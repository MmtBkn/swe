# Audio Guide

## Voiceover

A software GTM voiceover should sound like a trusted person describing a breakthrough, not a corporate narrator reading a launch blog.

Good VO traits:

- Clear
- Warm
- Controlled
- Slightly intimate
- Confident without hype
- Paced to leave room for comprehension

## Music

Music should carry the emotional arc:

1. Pressure or complexity
2. Lift or opening
3. Momentum
4. Resolve

Avoid over-scoring. Constant intensity makes the video feel generic and reduces the impact of key moments.

## Sound effects

Use SFX to make transitions and UI actions feel tactile. Keep them low in the mix and purposeful.

Useful SFX families:

- Soft UI confirmations
- Low pulses for pressure
- Gentle risers for transition
- Subtle impacts for reveal
- Air/space for visual openings

## Silence

Silence is a production tool. Use a small drop before the product proof or CTA when it helps attention snap into place.
